Hear this, O priests; listen, O house of Israel; pay attention, O house of the king; for judgment is upon you, because you have become a snare at Mizpah and a net spread upon Tabor.
The rebels are deeply intent on slaughter, even though I have rebuked them all.
I know Ephraim, and Israel is not hidden from me: for now, O Ephraim, you commit whoredom, and Israel is defiled.
They will not adjust their actions to turn back to their God: for the spirit of whoredoms is among them, and they have not known the LORD.
And the pride of Israel testifies against him: therefore, Israel and Ephraim shall fall in their iniquity; Judah shall also fall with them.
They will go with their flocks and herds to seek the LORD; but they will not find him; he has withdrawn himself from them.
They have acted treacherously against the LORD: for they have borne strange children; now a month will consume them with their portions.
Blow the horn in Gibeah, and the trumpet in Ramah; cry aloud at Bethaven, after you, O Benjamin.
Ephraim shall be desolate on the day of rebuke; among the tribes of Israel, I have made known what shall surely come to pass.
The princes of Judah are like those who remove the boundary; therefore, I will pour out my wrath upon them like water.
Ephraim is oppressed and broken in judgment, because he willingly followed the commandment.
Therefore, I will be to Ephraim like a moth, and to the house of Judah like rottenness.
When Ephraim saw his sickness, and Judah saw his wound, Ephraim went to the Assyrian and sent to King Jareb; yet he could not heal you, nor cure you of your wound.
For I will be to Ephraim like a lion, and like a young lion to the house of Judah: I, even I, will tear and go away; I will take away, and none shall rescue him.
I will go and return to my place until they acknowledge their offense and seek my face; in their affliction, they will seek me earnestly.
