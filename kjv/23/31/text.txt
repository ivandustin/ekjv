Woe to those who seek help from Egypt; who rely on horses and trust in chariots because of their numbers; and in horsemen because of their great strength; yet they do not look to the Holy One of Israel, nor do they seek the LORD!
However, He is wise and will bring calamity, and He will not retract His words: He will rise against the house of evildoers and against the assistance of those who commit iniquity.
Indeed, the Egyptians are mere men, not God; their horses are flesh, not spirit. When the LORD stretches out His hand, both the helper and the helped will fall, and they will all perish together.
For thus the LORD has spoken to me: Just as a lion and a young lion roar over their prey, when a multitude of shepherds is summoned against him, he will not be intimidated by their voices, nor will he lower himself because of their noise: so the LORD of hosts will come down to fight for Mount Zion and its hill.
Like birds in flight, the LORD of hosts will protect Jerusalem; He will defend it and deliver it, and He will preserve it as He passes over.
Turn to Him from whom the children of Israel have greatly revolted.
For on that day, every man will cast away his silver idols and his gold idols, which your own hands have made for sin.
Then the Assyrian will fall by the sword, not of a mighty man; and the sword, not of a common man, will consume him: he will flee from the sword, and his young men will be dismayed.
He will retreat to his stronghold in fear, and his princes will be terrified by the banner, says the LORD, whose fire is in Zion and whose furnace is in Jerusalem.
