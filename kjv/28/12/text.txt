Ephraim feeds on the wind and pursues the east wind; he continually increases lies and desolation. They have made a covenant with the Assyrians, and oil is transported to Egypt.  
The LORD has a dispute with Judah and will punish Jacob according to his actions; he will repay him based on his deeds.  
He grasped his brother's heel in the womb, and through his strength, he had power with God.  
Indeed, he had power over the angel and prevailed; he wept and made supplication to him. He found him in Bethel, and there he spoke with us.  
Even the LORD God of hosts; the LORD is his remembrance.  
Therefore, return to your God: maintain mercy and justice, and wait on your God continually.  
He is a merchant; deceitful balances are in his hand; he loves to oppress.  
And Ephraim said, "Yet I have become rich; I have found wealth for myself. In all my labors, they will find no iniquity in me that is sin."  
I, the LORD your God from the land of Egypt, will yet make you dwell in tents, as in the days of the solemn feast.  
I have also spoken through the prophets, multiplied visions, and used parables through the ministry of the prophets.  
Is there iniquity in Gilead? Surely they are worthless; they sacrifice bullocks in Gilgal; indeed, their altars are like heaps in the furrows of the fields.  
And Jacob fled to the land of Syria; Israel served for a wife, and for a wife, he kept sheep.  
By a prophet, the LORD brought Israel out of Egypt, and by a prophet, he was preserved.  
Ephraim provoked him to bitter anger; therefore, he will leave his blood upon him, and his reproach will the LORD return to him.
