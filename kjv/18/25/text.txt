Then Bildad the Shuhite responded and said,
Dominion and fear belong to Him; He brings peace in His lofty realms.
Is there any count to His armies? And upon whom does His light not shine?
How can a man be justified before God? Or how can one born of a woman be pure?
Behold, even the moon does not shine; indeed, the stars are not clean in His sight.
How much less is man, who is but a worm, and the son of man, who is merely a worm?
