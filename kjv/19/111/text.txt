Praise the LORD! I will wholeheartedly praise the LORD in the assembly of the upright and in the congregation.
The works of the LORD are magnificent, sought after by all who take pleasure in them.
His work is honorable and glorious; His righteousness endures forever.
He has made His wonderful works to be remembered; the LORD is gracious and full of compassion.
He provides food for those who fear Him; He will always be mindful of His covenant.
He has shown His people the power of His works, granting them the heritage of the nations.
The works of His hands are truth and justice; all His commandments are reliable.
They stand firm forever and ever, executed in truth and uprightness.
He sent redemption to His people; He has established His covenant forever; holy and revered is His name.
The fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom; a good understanding belongs to all who follow His commandments; His praise endures forever.
