His foundation lies in the sacred mountains.  
The LORD loves the gates of Zion more than all the dwellings of Jacob.  
Glorious things are proclaimed about you, O city of God. Selah.  
I will mention Rahab and Babylon to those who know me: look, Philistia and Tyre, along with Ethiopia; this man was born there.  
And it will be said of Zion, "This man and that man were born in her," and the Most High Himself will establish her.  
The LORD will record, when He registers the people, that this man was born there. Selah.  
Both the singers and the players of instruments will be present: all my springs are found in you.
