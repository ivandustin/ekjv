If his offering is a sacrifice of peace offering and he offers it from the herd, whether male or female, it must be without blemish before the LORD.  
He shall lay his hand on the head of the offering and kill it at the entrance of the tabernacle of the congregation; Aaron's sons, the priests, will sprinkle the blood around the altar.  
He shall present a portion of the peace offering as a fire offering to the LORD, including the fat that covers the innards and all the fat on the innards.  
He shall remove the two kidneys and the fat on them, located by the flanks, along with the caul above the liver, together with the kidneys.  
Aaron's sons will burn it on the altar, on top of the burnt sacrifice, which is on the wood in the fire; it is a fire offering, a pleasing aroma to the LORD.  
If his offering for a peace offering to the LORD is from the flock, whether male or female, it must be without blemish.  
If he offers a lamb, he shall present it before the LORD.  
He shall lay his hand on the head of the offering and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation; Aaron's sons will sprinkle its blood around the altar.  
He shall present a portion of the peace offering as a fire offering to the LORD, including the fat and the whole rump, which he shall remove near the backbone, along with the fat that covers the innards and all the fat on the innards.  
He shall also remove the two kidneys and the fat on them, located by the flanks, along with the caul above the liver, together with the kidneys.  
The priest shall burn these on the altar; it is the food of the fire offering to the LORD.  
If his offering is a goat, he shall present it before the LORD.  
He shall lay his hand on its head and kill it before the tabernacle of the congregation; Aaron's sons will sprinkle its blood around the altar.  
He shall present a portion of it as a fire offering to the LORD, including the fat that covers the innards and all the fat on the innards.  
He shall also remove the two kidneys and the fat on them, located by the flanks, along with the caul above the liver, together with the kidneys.  
The priest shall burn these on the altar; it is the food of the fire offering, a pleasing aroma; all the fat belongs to the LORD.  
This shall be a lasting statute for your generations in all your dwellings: you must not eat fat or blood.
