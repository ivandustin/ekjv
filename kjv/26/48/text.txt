These are the names of the tribes, starting from the northern end to the coast along the way of Hethlon, as one travels toward Hamath, Hazarenan, and the border of Damascus to the north, extending to the coast of Hamath; these define his boundaries to the east and west, with a portion designated for Dan.

Along the border of Dan, from the east side to the west side, there is a portion allocated for Asher.

Adjacent to the border of Asher, from the east side to the west side, a portion is set aside for Naphtali.

Next to the border of Naphtali, from the east side to the west side, a portion is designated for Manasseh.

Following the border of Manasseh, from the east side to the west side, a portion is allocated for Ephraim.

Along the border of Ephraim, from the east side to the west side, a portion is set aside for Reuben.

Next to the border of Reuben, from the east side to the west side, a portion is designated for Judah.

Along the border of Judah, from the east side to the west side, there shall be an offering that you will present, measuring twenty-five thousand reeds in width, and in length equal to the other portions, from the east side to the west side; the sanctuary shall be situated in the center of it.

The offering you present to the LORD shall measure twenty-five thousand in length and ten thousand in width.

For the priests, this holy offering shall be designated; to the north, it will measure twenty-five thousand in length, to the west ten thousand in width, to the east ten thousand in width, and to the south twenty-five thousand in length: the sanctuary of the LORD shall be in the center of this area.

This portion shall be for the priests who are sanctified from the sons of Zadok; they have kept my charge and did not stray when the children of Israel went astray, unlike the Levites.

This offering of land shall be considered most holy for them, adjacent to the border of the Levites.

Opposite the border of the priests, the Levites shall have a portion measuring twenty-five thousand in length and ten thousand in width: the total length shall be twenty-five thousand, and the width ten thousand.

They shall not sell, exchange, or alienate the firstfruits of the land, for it is holy to the LORD.

The remaining five thousand, which are left in width adjacent to the twenty-five thousand, shall be a common area for the city, for habitation and suburbs: the city shall be located in the center of this area.

These shall be the measurements: the north side shall be four thousand five hundred, the south side four thousand five hundred, the east side four thousand five hundred, and the west side four thousand five hundred.

The suburbs of the city shall extend two hundred fifty units to the north, two hundred fifty to the south, two hundred fifty to the east, and two hundred fifty to the west.

The remaining length, adjacent to the offering of the holy portion, shall be ten thousand to the east and ten thousand to the west: it shall be opposite the offering of the holy portion, and its produce shall provide food for those who serve the city.

Those who serve the city shall come from all the tribes of Israel.

The total offering shall measure twenty-five thousand by twenty-five thousand: you shall present the holy offering as a square, along with the city's possession.

The remaining area shall belong to the prince, on both sides of the holy offering and the city's possession, opposite the twenty-five thousand of the offering toward the east border, and westward opposite the twenty-five thousand toward the west border, adjacent to the portions for the prince: this shall be the holy offering, and the sanctuary of the house shall be in the center of it.

Furthermore, from the possession of the Levites and the possession of the city, which is situated in the midst of the prince's land, between the borders of Judah and Benjamin, shall belong to the prince.

As for the remaining tribes, from the east side to the west side, Benjamin shall receive a portion.

Adjacent to the border of Benjamin, from the east side to the west side, Simeon shall receive a portion.

Next to the border of Simeon, from the east side to the west side, Issachar shall receive a portion.

Following the border of Issachar, from the east side to the west side, Zebulun shall receive a portion.

Next to the border of Zebulun, from the east side to the west side, Gad shall receive a portion.

Along the border of Gad, on the southern side, the border shall extend from Tamar to the waters of strife in Kadesh, and to the river leading to the great sea.

This is the land that you shall divide by lot among the tribes of Israel for their inheritance, and these are their portions, says the Lord GOD.

These are the exits of the city on the north side, measuring four thousand five hundred.

The gates of the city shall bear the names of the tribes of Israel: three gates to the north; one gate for Reuben, one gate for Judah, and one gate for Levi.

On the east side, measuring four thousand five hundred, there shall be three gates; one gate for Joseph, one gate for Benjamin, and one gate for Dan.

On the south side, measuring four thousand five hundred, there shall be three gates; one gate for Simeon, one gate for Issachar, and one gate for Zebulun.

On the west side, measuring four thousand five hundred, there shall be three gates; one gate for Gad, one gate for Asher, and one gate for Naphtali.

The total perimeter shall be eighteen thousand measures: and from that day, the name of the city shall be, "The LORD is there."
