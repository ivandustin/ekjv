And the LORD spoke to Moses and Aaron, saying,
Each man of the children of Israel shall camp by his own standard, with the emblem of their father's house: they shall camp at a distance around the tabernacle of the congregation.
On the east side, toward the rising sun, the standard of the camp of Judah shall be positioned with their armies: Nahshon the son of Amminadab shall be the captain of the children of Judah.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered sixty-four thousand six hundred.
Next to him shall camp the tribe of Issachar: Nethaneel the son of Zuar shall be the captain of the children of Issachar.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered fifty-four thousand four hundred.
Then comes the tribe of Zebulun: Eliab the son of Helon shall be the captain of the children of Zebulun.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered fifty-seven thousand four hundred.
All those counted in the camp of Judah totaled one hundred eighty-six thousand four hundred; these shall set out first.
On the south side shall be the standard of the camp of Reuben according to their armies: Elizur the son of Shedeur shall be the captain of the children of Reuben.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered forty-six thousand five hundred.
Next to him shall camp the tribe of Simeon: Shelumiel the son of Zurishaddai shall be the captain of the children of Simeon.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered fifty-nine thousand three hundred.
Then comes the tribe of Gad: Eliasaph the son of Reuel shall be the captain of the sons of Gad.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered forty-five thousand six hundred fifty.
All those counted in the camp of Reuben totaled one hundred fifty-one thousand four hundred fifty; they shall set out in the second rank.
Then the tabernacle of the congregation shall move forward with the camp of the Levites in the center of the camp: as they encamp, so shall they move forward, each man in his place by their standards.
On the west side shall be the standard of the camp of Ephraim according to their armies: Elishama the son of Ammihud shall be the captain of the sons of Ephraim.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered forty thousand five hundred.
Next to him shall camp the tribe of Manasseh: Gamaliel the son of Pedahzur shall be the captain of the children of Manasseh.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered thirty-two thousand two hundred.
Then comes the tribe of Benjamin: Abidan the son of Gideoni shall be the captain of the sons of Benjamin.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered thirty-five thousand four hundred.
All those counted in the camp of Ephraim totaled one hundred eight thousand one hundred; they shall move forward in the third rank.
The standard of the camp of Dan shall be on the north side according to their armies: Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai shall be the captain of the children of Dan.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered sixty-two thousand seven hundred.
Next to him shall camp the tribe of Asher: Pagiel the son of Ocran shall be the captain of the children of Asher.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered forty-one thousand five hundred.
Then comes the tribe of Naphtali: Ahira the son of Enan shall be the captain of the children of Naphtali.
His host, and those counted among them, numbered fifty-three thousand four hundred.
All those counted in the camp of Dan totaled one hundred fifty-seven thousand six hundred; they shall move last with their standards.
These are the numbers of the children of Israel by the house of their fathers: all those counted in the camps throughout their hosts totaled six hundred three thousand five hundred fifty.
But the Levites were not counted among the children of Israel, as the LORD commanded Moses.
And the children of Israel did according to all that the LORD commanded Moses: they camped by their standards and set out, each one after their families, according to the house of their fathers.
