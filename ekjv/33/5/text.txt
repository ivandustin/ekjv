Now gather yourselves in troops, O daughter of troops: he has laid siege against us; they will strike the judge of Israel with a rod upon the cheek.
But you, Bethlehem Ephratah, though you are small among the thousands of Judah, out of you shall come forth one who is to be ruler in Israel; whose origins are from ancient times, from everlasting.
Therefore, he will give them up until the time that she who is in labor has given birth; then the remnant of his brothers shall return to the children of Israel.
He shall stand and shepherd in the strength of the LORD, in the majesty of the name of the LORD his God; and they shall dwell securely, for now he shall be great to the ends of the earth.
This man shall be our peace when the Assyrian invades our land; when he marches into our palaces, we will raise against him seven shepherds and eight leaders.
They shall devastate the land of Assyria with the sword, and the land of Nimrod at its entrances; thus, he will deliver us from the Assyrian when he comes into our land and when he treads within our borders.
The remnant of Jacob shall be in the midst of many peoples like dew from the LORD, like showers on the grass, which do not wait for man nor linger for the sons of men.
The remnant of Jacob shall be among the nations in the midst of many peoples like a lion among the beasts of the forest, like a young lion among flocks of sheep; who, if he goes through, treads down and tears in pieces, and no one can rescue.
Your hand shall be lifted up against your adversaries, and all your enemies shall be cut off.
It shall come to pass in that day, says the LORD, that I will cut off your horses from your midst, and I will destroy your chariots.
I will cut off the cities of your land and demolish all your strongholds.
I will eliminate witchcraft from your hand, and you shall no longer have soothsayers.
I will also cut off your carved images and your sacred pillars from your midst; you shall no longer worship the work of your hands.
I will uproot your groves from your midst; thus, I will destroy your cities.
I will execute vengeance in anger and fury upon the nations, such as they have not heard.
